Through the FIRST Scholarship Program, FIRST participants from across the world have access to \$80 million worth in scholarship opportunities from nearly 200 hundred Colleges and Universities. Since 2015, [Macquarie University](https://www.mq.edu.au/) has provided a number of scholarships available to high-achieving FIRST Robotics participants who apply for an undergraduate course within the Faculty of Science and Engineering at Macquarie University.

Macquarie University is ranked among the top 1% of world universities and is recognised globally as a leading university. Based in Sydney, Australia, Macquarie University established [FIRST® Australia](http://firstaustralia.org/) in 2006 and is the home of the Thunder Down Under.

[Learn more about the FIRST® Robotics Scholarship at Macquarie University](https://www.mq.edu.au/study/articles/macquarie-university-first-robotics-scholarship).
